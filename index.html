I am passionate about product, strategy, and design implementation. Let's make something together.

 

Design is passion of mine stemming from my love of visual arts and shaped by my industrial design background. My professional design career has included a position at NASA Johnson Space Center as a habitat designer, a lead furniture designer and product manager at Rottet Studios in Houston, and now my continuing education at Carnegie Mellon perusing my masters in Human Computer Interaction.

I consider myself an interaction designer who questions the changing paradigms of technology and society. An individual who looks past just the beginnings of a product - but thinks about life cycle that a product ensues and how we can utilize strategy to create lasting solutions.
